Our Story

Born from love
of arcades

TIE-Games is an indie studio based in Dresden, Germany. We grew up with classic arcade games - the kind that taught you through play, where one more try always felt within reach.

Our games are built around three principles: respect the player's time, keep controls simple, and make every level a memorable moment. No tutorials that won't end. No notifications begging you to come back. Just games worth coming back to.
