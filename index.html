Our Story

Born from love
of arcades

TIE-Games is an independent studio based in Dresden, Germany. We grew up with classic arcade games - the kind that taught you through play, where one more try always felt within reach.

Our games respect the player. No tutorials that won't end. No notifications begging you to come back. No data harvesting under the guise of personalization.
