Privacy Policy for Evolrace

Effective date: April 28, 2026
Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Evolrace ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Evolrace itself does not directly collect personal information from users. However, the following third-party services integrated into the App may collect data:

1.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location based on IP
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used by Google for ad personalization, fraud prevention, and analytics. For details, see the Google Privacy Policy and AdMob policies.

1.2 Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

If you choose to purchase "Remove Ads" or other in-app products, Google Play Billing processes the transaction. We do not collect or store your payment information. Google Play handles all financial transactions according to their privacy policy.

1.3 Google Play Services

The App relies on Google Play Services for core functionality (app installation, billing, ads). Google Play Services may collect device information as described in their privacy policy.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores limited data locally on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" option
  • Your game progress and high score (if applicable)
  • Game preferences and settings

This data remains entirely on your device and is not transmitted to any server controlled by us. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

4. Your Choices and Rights

4.1 Opt Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization (or Reset advertising ID)

4.2 Remove Ads

If you wish to remove all advertisements, you can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from within the App.

4.3 Data Deletion

To remove locally stored data, simply uninstall the App from your device. For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy controls.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Data collected by integrated third-party services (Google AdMob, Google Play) is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Security

All data transmitted between the App and third-party services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. While we and our service providers take reasonable measures to protect data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. International Users

The App may be used worldwide. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of any data to, and processing in, countries where our service providers operate, which may include the United States and other countries with different privacy laws than your own.

8. GDPR (European Users)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete data, and the right to object to or restrict processing. For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's GDPR resources.

9. CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what data is collected and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at this URL. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
